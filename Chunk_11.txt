11. Chunk
Chunk title: EPA fuel-cost and emissions tools

Source title: FuelEconomy.gov — Fuel Economy home page

Source URL: https://fueleconomy.gov/

 

Summary

FuelEconomy.gov includes tools such as a Fuel Savings Calculator, Trip Calculator, Used Car Label Tool, GHG Emissions Calculator, and Plug-in Hybrid Calculator. Cars.ai should use this chunk when a user’s question is really about ownership cost, commuting, or road-trip practicality. This chunk helps the assistant move beyond raw MPG into more decision-friendly language, such as likely fuel savings, emissions comparison, and travel-use planning. For long-term ownership questions, the assistant should note that efficiency affects operating cost year after year. For hybrid and plug-in hybrid questions, Cars.ai can use this chunk to explain that official calculators exist to estimate whether the added technology may save money in the user’s driving pattern.
