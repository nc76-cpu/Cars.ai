19. Chunk
Chunk title: Extended warranty caution

Source title: FTC — Extended Warranties and Service Contracts

Source URL: https://consumer.ftc.gov/articles/extended-warranties-and-service-contracts

 

Summary

The FTC says an extended warranty or service contract might not be worth the cost if the product is not likely to need repairs, and consumers should compare the contract against the warranty they already have. The FTC also warns that the company behind the contract may not be the manufacturer and advises checking the provider’s reputation and the specific coverage limits. Cars.ai should use this chunk to help users decide whether an added protection plan makes a car deal better or worse. This chunk is especially important for “bad deal” detection: an expensive add-on with limited extra coverage can weaken an otherwise decent purchase.
