3. Chunk
Chunk title: NHTSA recall lookup basics

Source title: NHTSA — Check for Recalls: Vehicle, Car Seat, Tire, Equipment

Source URL: https://www.nhtsa.gov/recalls

 

Summary

NHTSA’s recall search allows consumers to check vehicles, tires, car seats, and equipment for safety recalls. A VIN or license-plate search can show whether a specific vehicle needs to be repaired as part of a recall. A year-make-model search gives broader results for recalls, investigations, complaints, and manufacturer communications. Cars.ai should use this chunk whenever a user asks whether a car is a risky buy or whether a specific used car should be avoided. An open recall does not automatically mean the car is permanently bad, but it does create an immediate caution flag because the safety issue needs to be repaired. This chunk is also useful for teaching users the difference between model-level research and VIN-level research: year-make-model gives general patterns, while VIN gives vehicle-specific recall status.
