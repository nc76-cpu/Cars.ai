6. Chunk
Chunk title: IIHS rating framework

Source title: IIHS — Vehicle ratings

Source URL: https://www.iihs.org/ratings

 

Summary

IIHS explains that its vehicle ratings cover two broad areas: crashworthiness, meaning how well a vehicle protects occupants in a crash, and crash avoidance and mitigation, meaning technology that can prevent a crash or reduce its severity. Cars.ai should use this chunk to help users understand why IIHS information complements NHTSA instead of replacing it. When a user asks whether one car is safer than another, the assistant can explain that safety includes both protection during a crash and the systems designed to help avoid one. This chunk is particularly useful for buyers comparing newer vehicles with advanced driver-assistance features. It also supports a more nuanced answer than simply saying a vehicle is “safe” or “unsafe,” because the assistant can separate occupant protection from crash-avoidance technology.
