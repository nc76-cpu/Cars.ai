9. Chunk
Chunk title: EPA/FuelEconomy.gov vehicle comparison entry point

Source title: FuelEconomy.gov — Find and Compare Cars

Source URL: https://www.fueleconomy.gov/feg/findacar.shtml

 

Summary

FuelEconomy.gov provides a Find and Compare Cars tool that lets users search by MPG, price, make, body style, and related characteristics. Cars.ai should use this chunk to frame fuel economy as a structured comparison category rather than an afterthought. When a user asks which car is better for long-term ownership, fuel cost belongs in the answer because the purchase price alone does not represent the full ownership picture. This chunk is especially valuable for users choosing between gas, hybrid, plug-in hybrid, and electric vehicles, because it creates a consistent way to compare efficiency across options. The assistant should remind users that strong fuel economy may make a vehicle more attractive over time, but it should still be weighed alongside safety, recall status, warranty clarity, and the user’s driving habits.
