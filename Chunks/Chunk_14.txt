14. Chunk
Chunk title: Vehicle history is not enough without inspection

Source title: FTC — Buying a Used Car From a Dealer

Source URL: https://consumer.ftc.gov/articles/buying-used-car-dealer

 

Summary

The FTC says a vehicle history report is not a substitute for an independent vehicle inspection. The agency notes that history reports may show accidents and flood damage but typically do not show mechanical problems. Cars.ai should use this chunk whenever a user asks if a used car is “safe to buy” based only on a history report or dealer certification. This chunk supports cautious, evidence-based answers: even a car with a clean report may still have serious mechanical issues. The assistant should recommend combining a history report with an independent mechanic’s inspection, recall lookup, and VIN verification. For Cars.ai, this chunk is one of the clearest examples of what the corpus can and cannot prove about a specific used car.
