2. Chunk
Chunk title: NHTSA comparison tool includes ratings and recalls

Source title: NHTSA — Car Safety Ratings | Vehicles, Car Seats, Tires

Source URL: https://www.nhtsa.gov/ratings

 

Summary

NHTSA directs shoppers to its vehicle comparison tool to view 5-Star Safety Ratings and recall information at a glance. This matters for Cars.ai because many users ask broad questions such as “Which car is better?” A strong response should turn that broad request into a comparison workflow: check official crash ratings, then check recall exposure, then combine that with cost and purchase-risk information from other sources. This chunk supports answers that recommend a comparison process rather than a single unsupported opinion. It is especially useful when the user names two or three candidate vehicles and wants a structured buying decision. The assistant should present safety and recall status as separate categories, because a vehicle can have acceptable crash performance and still need caution if it has open recalls or a poor recall history.
