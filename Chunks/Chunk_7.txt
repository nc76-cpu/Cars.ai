7. Chunk
Chunk title: IIHS 2026 Top Safety Pick+ criteria

Source title: IIHS — 2026 Top Safety Picks

Source URL: https://www.iihs.org/ratings/top-safety-picks

 

Summary

For 2026, IIHS says Top Safety Pick+ vehicles must earn good ratings in the small overlap front, moderate overlap front, and side tests, plus acceptable or good headlights, good pedestrian front crash prevention with standard availability, and acceptable or good vehicle-to-vehicle front crash prevention 2.0 with standard availability. Cars.ai should use this chunk when a user wants to know why one new vehicle stands out in current crash testing. This chunk supports strong positive recommendations for vehicles that meet demanding safety thresholds. It also helps the assistant explain why a model that lacks the award is not automatically a bad car; it may simply fall short on one requirement. The value of the chunk is that it turns a badge or award into a clear set of measurable safety conditions
