5. Chunk
Chunk title: NHTSA VIN decoder for vehicle identification

Source title: NHTSA — VIN Decoder

Source URL: https://www.nhtsa.gov/vin-decoder

 

Summary

NHTSA provides a public VIN decoder that lets a user query a vehicle identification number to identify information encoded in the VIN. Cars.ai should use this chunk when a shopper wants to verify exactly what vehicle they are considering before checking recalls, specifications, or other records. This is especially useful in used-car shopping, where trim, drivetrain, model year, and manufacturing details can affect value and eligibility for other checks. The assistant should explain that VIN verification is a first step, not the final answer: after confirming identity, the user should still check recalls, safety ratings, inspection results, and purchase documents. This chunk is valuable because it helps prevent comparing the wrong configuration or trusting a seller’s listing without verifying what the VIN actually represents.
